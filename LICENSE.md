## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is this open source?

Ollama Cluster is **source-available**, not OSI open source. The code is public and contributions are welcome, but use by larger for-profit organisations requires a Commercial License.

### Can I use it commercially on the Free License?

Yes — if you meet the eligibility criteria (individual, non-profit, or for-profit organisation with annual gross revenue of AUD $100,000 or less).

### How is "annual gross revenue" defined?

Total gross revenue of the legal entity using the software, measured in Australian dollars over the most recent twelve-month period, including consolidated revenue of related entities under common ownership or control. When in doubt, contact the copyright holder.

### We are an agency deploying this for a client. Who needs a license?

The entity that owns or operates the deployment in production is responsible for compliance. If the combined relevant organisation exceeds the revenue threshold, a Commercial License is required.

### Can I contribute without a Commercial License?

Yes. Contributions are welcome from anyone under the terms described in [CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md). Contributors grant rights to their contributions so the project can be distributed under this license.

### What if my team grows past the threshold?

Organisations that initially qualify for the Free License must obtain a Commercial License once they exceed the revenue threshold.
