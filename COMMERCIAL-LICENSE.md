# Commercial License

A Commercial License is required for for-profit organisations that do not qualify for the [Free License](LICENSE.md), typically those with **annual gross revenue above AUD $100,000**.

## What you get

A Commercial License allows your organisation to:

- deploy and operate Ollama Cluster in production
- modify the software for internal use
- receive agreed support and compliance terms (where included in your agreement)

Pricing and terms are negotiated based on your organisation's size and use case.

## How to request a license

Contact the copyright holder to discuss terms:

- **GitHub:** [github.com/levi-putna/ollama-cluster/issues](https://github.com/levi-putna/ollama-cluster/issues) (open an issue titled "Commercial License enquiry")
- **Repository:** [github.com/levi-putna/ollama-cluster](https://github.com/levi-putna/ollama-cluster)

Please include:

1. Organisation name and country
2. Approximate annual gross revenue (AUD)
3. Intended use (internal, SaaS, managed service, etc.)
4. Approximate number of nodes or deployments

We aim to respond within a few business days.

## Evaluation

Organisations evaluating Ollama Cluster before a production rollout may use the Free License during evaluation. A Commercial License is required before sustained production use once you exceed the revenue threshold.
